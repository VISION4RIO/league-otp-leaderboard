## About

League OTP Leaderboard is an advanced League of Legends analytics platform focused on identifying the world's best champion specialists.

Instead of relying only on LP, win rate or KDA, the project combines official Riot Games API data with a proprietary ranking methodology designed to reward long-term consistency and champion mastery.

The objective is to provide players with a more meaningful way to discover the strongest one-tricks and champion specialists across every Riot region.

---

## Why?

Current ranking websites are excellent tools for general statistics, but champion-specific leaderboards often have limitations.

Small sample sizes, temporary win streaks or smurf accounts can significantly influence rankings.

League OTP Leaderboard was created to prioritize consistent performance over time rather than isolated results.

---

## Vision

Create the most accurate champion specialist ranking platform available for League of Legends.

Our goal is to become the reference website for discovering high-level players dedicated to a specific champion.

## Champion Performance Rating (CPR)

The project introduces the **Champion Performance Rating (CPR)**.

Rather than using a single metric, CPR evaluates multiple aspects of player performance to produce a balanced ranking score.

Examples include:

* Ranked Performance
* Champion Experience
* Match Consistency
* Long-Term Results
* Individual Impact
* Performance Trends
* Champion Dedication
* Sample Size Reliability

The exact weighting is continuously refined throughout development.
